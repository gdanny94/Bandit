About us

At Bandit our mission is simple: to keep you riding smoothly and safely. We believe in the freedom and joy that cycling brings, and our goal is to support that by offering reliable, high-quality bike repair services. We are dedicated to helping every cyclist, from daily commuters to weekend adventures, maintain their bikes in peak condition.

Our Story

Bandit was founded by a group of cyclists who saw a need for a community-focused repair shop that combined expert service with a welcoming atmosphere. Our name "Bandit" reflects the spirit of adventure and independence that cycling embodies. We wanted to create a place where cyclists feel at home and confident that their bikes are in the best hands.
